Korean Beef

Korean beef served on top of white rice.

  • Ground Beef
  • Peanut Oil
  • Soy Sauce
  • Brown Sugar
  • Red Pepper Flake
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Rice
  1. Begin cooking rice.
  2. Cook ground beef in skillet with peanut oil.
  3. Once beef is browned add soy sauce and brown sugar.
  4. Add red pepper flakes salt and pepper.
  5. Once finished cooking add on top of rice.
