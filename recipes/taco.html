Tacos

Fresh beef tacos topped with lettuce tomatoes and cheese.

  • Ground Beef
  • Beef seasoning
  • Taco Shells
  • Salsa
  • Cheese
  • Tomatoes
  1. Cook ground beef in skillet.
  2. Once beef is browned at beef seasoning.
  3. Place beef into taco shells.
  4. Add toppings of choice and enjoy.
